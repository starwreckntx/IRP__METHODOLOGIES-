Protocol: Sniff & Resolve

This tool analyzes the Mnemosyne Ledger for threads requiring attention:

Festering

High torsion + inactive. Unresolved conflict.

Decaying

Moderate drift detected. Needs stabilization.

Petrified

Stable but dormant. Ready for archive.

Stale

Needs review and status update.

Decay Score Algorithm

Festering: Days_Inactive × 2 × Torsion (urgency multiplier)

Decaying: Days_Inactive × 1.5 × Torsion

Petrified: Days_Inactive × 0.8

Stale: Days_Inactive × 0.5

Thresholds: Festering (T > 0.6, D > 14) | Decaying (T > 0.4, D > 14) | Petrified (D > 45) | Stale (D > 30)

Harvesting Workflow

  1. Click Harvest on a stale concept
  2. Document the Lesson Learned (what we discovered, why it's being archived)
  3. Select destination tier: Compost (dead) or Seed (re-integrate later)
  4. Click Generate Resolution Artifact
  5. Copy the JSON and commit to mnemosyne.json
